# Sitemap: Viral-Flow AI Platform

## 1. Marketing & Onboarding
- **Landing Page (/)**: Value proposition, "Viralize" demo, pricing, and CTA.
- **Authentication (/auth)**: Sign up, Login, Password Recovery.
- **Onboarding Flow (/onboarding)**: Creator niche selection (e.g., Podcast, Gaming, Education) to tune the AI highlight engine.

## 2. Main Workspace (Dashboard)
- **Project Dashboard (/dashboard)**: List of uploaded long-form videos and their status.
- **Upload Center (/upload)**: Drag-and-drop or URL import (YouTube/Twitch).

## 3. The Highlight Studio (Core Feature)
- **Highlight Discovery (/project/[id]/highlights)**: 
    - AI-generated grid of "Viral Candidates."
    - Viral Score (0-100) per clip.
    - Justification (Why the AI picked this).
- **Short-Form Editor (/project/[id]/edit/[clip_id])**:
    - **Auto-Reframe**: Switch between original, 9:16 (Center), and Split Screen.
    - **Caption Engine**: Dynamic word-by-word styling.
    - **B-Roll Layer**: AI-suggested stock footage or images.
    - **Audio/Music**: Ducking and trending background tracks.

## 4. Management & Export
- **Export Queue (/exports)**: List of rendering clips and download links.
- **Direct Social Integration**: One-click publish to TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts.
- **Settings (/settings)**: API integrations, Branding kits (Fonts/Colors), Subscription management.
