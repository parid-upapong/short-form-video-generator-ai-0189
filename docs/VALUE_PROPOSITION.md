# Viral Value Proposition

## The Problem
Content creators spend 80% of their time on tedious "manual labor" (cutting dead air, centering faces, adding subtitles) and only 20% on actual creativity. This leads to burnout and inconsistent posting.

## The "Viral-Flow" Solution
We provide an AI-powered co-pilot that handles the technical grunt work, allowing creators to focus on the message.

| Feature | Old Way (Manual) | Viral-Flow Way (AI) |
|---------|------------------|---------------------|
| **Highlight Finding** | Watching 1-hour footage (60 mins) | AI Scan (30 seconds) |
| **Reframing (9:16)** | Manual keyframing of moving subjects | Auto-Face Tracking AI |
| **Subtitles** | Typing and syncing manually | Dynamic, animated templates |
| **Hooks** | Guessing what works | Data-driven hook suggestions |

## Viral Value Hook
"Don't just post. Dominate. Turn your 60-minute podcast into 15 viral clips before your coffee gets cold."
